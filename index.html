Paramount Plus Login



Activate Paramount Plus from Paramount plus.com/activate code. On receiving Paramountplus.com login code, visit your web browser and then go to paramountplus.com/roku and move ahead. Now, put in your Paramount Plus login credentials. Activate Paramount Plus on Roku.



Way to Create Paramount Plus account


For Paramount Plus login, you should possess an account.

  1. Start by adding Paramount Plus Channel on your Roku.
  2. Then launch the Channel for Paramount Plus sign up.
  3. Now, select any Subscription Plan.
  4. Thereafter, tap on the "Create Account" section. Then tap onm the 'Next' button.
  5. Lastly, put in your Roku PIN.
  6. On successful Sign up, you can now sign in Paramount plus.
  7. Further, tap on 'On My TV'.
  8. This will land you to paramount plus.com/account and sign in screen.
  9. Eventually enter the Email Id and Password. Then, tap on the 'Sign In' button.



Install Paramount Channel on Roku TV

  1. On Roku Remote tap on Home button.
  2. Nextly, choose the Roku Channel.
  3. Now, Subscription screen launches.
  4. Then, browse the Subscription plans offered to you.
  5. Now, choose the Paramount Plus channel.
  6. Launching the Paramount Plus channel on Roku.
  7. Select the 'Sign Up' button.
  8. On 'Create Account' screen you will see option to sign up Paramount Plus account.
  9. Now, put in your Email Address and Password.
  10. Further, choose a Subscription plan.
  11. Thereafter, enter your Roku PIN.
  12. Tap on the 'Next' button.
  13. This will land you to the Homescreen, where you need to simply sign In your account.

Enter Provider Code

  1. Head on to Paramount App and tap on "Sign In With A Partner".
  2. Then, choose your TV Provider and you'll get Activation Code.
  3. Now, head on to paramountplus.com/activate on your device and enter Activation Code.
  4. If you want to login Paramount Plus from website, tap 'Sign In with a Partner'.
  5. Here, choose your TV Provider.
  6. Put in your Username and Password.
  7. In case you've not created account then you must proceed ahead with Paramount Plus Sign Up.
  8. For Paramount Login on Mobile device, you need to download and install Paramount Plus app.
